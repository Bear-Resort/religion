## Existence of Time

Before one shall concern the creations from the God, the scope of time shall be naturally explored. Rather than having time being created, we endorse that time exists consistently, in the format of $\mathbb{R}/ 2\pi \cong \mathbb{R} \cup \{\infty\}$.

Some diligent readers might ask: Why do we format time in this format? This construction somewhat correlates with the general projective space, so that $+\infty$ and $-\infty$ coincides. 

In this construction, there will be two definitions for time, the time designated to a local scale and the time designated to a global scale.

- The local scale of time works exactly like we interpret time, the length of an hour feels exactly the same now or compared to 10 years ago.  
- The global scale of time marks the global view of time, there exists a time $\infty$ (on local scale) or $0 \equiv 2\pi$ (on global scale) that is never reachable within finitely amount of time.

The conversion between the local and global time is given by the following formula $\varphi:\mathbb{R} \cup \{\infty\}\to \mathbb{R}/2\pi$:

$$
    \varphi(t_{\mathrm{loc}}) = \begin{cases}
        2\arctan(t_{\mathrm{loc}}) + \pi, & \text{ if } t_{\mathrm{loc}} \neq \infty,\\
        0, & \text{ if } t_{\mathrm{loc}} = \infty.
    \end{cases}
$$

Specifically, we note that $\infty = -\infty$ and $\varphi$ is clearly invertible, thus we can easily correlate the two systems of times. Meanwhile, we would define that time naturally exists, it never started to exist, but it rather exists as a cycle.

## Existence of the God

With our construction of time, the God naturally exists. Since time is cyclic, the God also never started to exist, she existed as herself at time $t_{\mathrm{loc}} = \infty$ or $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$ just as how she existed earlier.

Over such construction of time, our God and the matters of the world consistently exist. The God remains herself but the matters re-ensembles and re-combines into entities of the world, obeying the following laws:

- None entities ensemble by the matters (other than the God) can exist over a time span infinitely over the local time span, *i.e.*, none entities other than our God can cross over the global time $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0 \equiv 2\pi$ as destiny.
- At global time $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$, all matters are concentrated at a singular point, which is where the world started to develop. This is when the world had the most order and least entropy.
- Since global time $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$, all matters would start to expand, *i.e.*, expanding in all directions on the long term scale. Eventually when the global time increases towards $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 2\pi$ (which is our origin of time), they accumulate again to singularity (this will be explained in full details of how the God constructed the space axes).

Thus, the global time of $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$ is the most ordered space, but there is no significance of entity other than the God. With the existence of time and our God, we will explore on how she builds up the world through the span of times in the upcoming chapters.
