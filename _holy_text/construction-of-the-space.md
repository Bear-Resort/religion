## Creation of the Space

From the last section, we have mentioned that the matters concentrates at a singular point, and this is where our world started to be created.

At the singularity time $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$, all matter and mass are concentrated the singular point, and this point serves as the origin of the space. This position is the **unique identity** of the space.

From $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$, the God completed her construction of all dimensions of the space over a finite amount of time in the local spectrum, but recall that time was expanded infinitely since the singularity, the construction was done at some $t_{\mathrm{loc}} = -\infty + C = \infty + C = \infty = -\infty$, and on the global timescale, the completion is still done at $t_{\mathrm{glb}} = 0$. The completion of the space allows everything to be developed thereafter. Particularly, her constructions were done in two steps:

1. The first step is to construct the first axis other than the time axis, so matters starts to scatter in a single direction.
2. The next step is to construct the other two axes simultaneously, so that matters can scatter in three orthogonal direction.
3. Although there are other orthogonal directions, the God prohibits matters to be distributed in the space, and she worked on locking these directions, while creating additional orders to the world.
